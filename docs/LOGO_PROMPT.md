# Logo generation prompt (enhanced for this codebase)

Use the prompt below with an image/logo generator (e.g. DALL·E, Midjourney, Ideogram, or Stitch) to produce a logo that matches the Carrie A. Yu portfolio site.

---

## Enhanced prompt (copy-paste ready)

**A minimal, professional software-developer logo that reads clearly at small sizes (favicon/header) and works as a circular avatar, with a clean tech feel and no clip-art clutter.**

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- **Platform / use:** Web portfolio; logo appears in header (≈40×40px), hero as circular avatar (128×128px), favicon, and OpenGraph.
- **Theme:** Light-first, professional, trustworthy. Must also work on dark backgrounds (single-color or inverted variant).
- **Background:** Transparent or white (#ffffff) for light; logo should have a variant that works on dark (#111827 or #1a1a2e).
- **Primary accent:** Blue (#2563eb) and/or deeper blue (#1d4ed8) for primary mark or accent—aligned with site’s blue-50/indigo-100 hero gradient and blue-600 headings.
- **Secondary / neutrals:** Dark gray (#111827) or near-black for wordmark or icon; optional soft gray (#4b5563) for secondary elements. Avoid loud or playful colors.
- **Shape language:** Slightly rounded (border-radius feel ~10px), geometric and precise—no hand-drawn or cartoon style. Clean edges, limited detail so it scales down to favicon size.
- **Typography (if wordmark):** Clean, modern sans-serif (Inter-like); professional weight (medium/semibold). No script or decorative fonts.

**LOGO STRUCTURE:**
1. **Mark / icon:** Single, simple symbol or monogram that reads at 16×16px and 40×40px. Options: abstract “C” or “CAY” monogram, minimal code bracket `</>`, or a subtle geometric shape (e.g. circuit/node, minimal “dev” abstraction). Prefer one clear idea; avoid busy combinations.
2. **Format:** Square or circular-safe (important content within a circle for avatar use). No thin strokes that disappear at small size; sufficient contrast on white and on dark.
3. **Style:** Flat or very subtle depth (e.g. one soft shadow). No 3D, gradients (or one very subtle gradient), or photorealism. Fits a developer/portfolio brand: competent, approachable, modern.
4. **Deliverables to consider:** One primary version (e.g. blue + dark gray on transparent), plus an inverted or single-color version for dark mode / favicon.

**MOOD KEYWORDS:** minimal, professional, developer, portfolio, trustworthy, clean, tech-forward, scalable, recognizable at small size, no clip-art, no generic “tech globe”.

**CONSTRAINTS:** Must be suitable for a personal software developer portfolio (Carrie A. Yu). Avoid stock imagery, globes, generic rockets, or overused tech clichés. Prefer distinctive but simple so it feels owned by the brand.

---

**Optional:** If you use this with Stitch or another UI tool, you can add: “Output as SVG or high-resolution PNG (e.g. 512×512 or 1024×1024) with transparent background.”

---

💡 **Tip:** For consistent branding across the site (buttons, cards, other assets), consider adding a `DESIGN.md` with the same color tokens and style so future assets stay aligned.
